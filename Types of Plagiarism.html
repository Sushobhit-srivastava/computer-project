Different types of Plagiarism

Direct Plagiarism This type of PLagiarism occurs when the user copies the work of another person, word to word, without acknowledging the original author or quoting the text.
Self Plagiarism It is the type of plagiarism where the author uses his own original text in more than one contexts without citing that it was used earlier also. the reader is made to believe that the text is originalin every context that it is used. Even though this act is not illegal, it is definitely not ethical.
Mosaic Plagiarism This type of plagiarism happens when the same sentence structure is used with synonyms for the appropriate words of the sentence.
Acciental Plagiarism It is when the user does not really understand how to properly paraphrase, quote, and cite the research.
